Origin Story

Senior control,
agentic speed.

We saw early that agentic engineering can accelerate delivery dramatically, but only if an experienced person stays in control of architecture, quality, and business trade-offs. That model changes team economics: less junior-heavy coding effort, faster outcomes, and lower delivery overhead.

What Changed

Programming speed went up. Supervision depth became more important.

  • Agents can now execute large technical workloads quickly, including most implementation tasks.
  • Experienced expertise is still essential for framing the business problem and setting technical constraints.
  • Quality, reliability, and explainability improve when humans remain in the decision loop.
  • Security, privacy, and governance are designed in from day one, including GDPR-aware handling of operational data.
  • Auditability, access control, and controlled release processes are part of delivery, not afterthoughts.
  • The result is a leaner delivery model with faster iteration and lower total project cost.

Control Loop

How we build with agents

1. Experienced framing

2. Agentic execution

Rapid coding, data preparation, experiments, and tooling integration.

3. Human validation

Review, challenge assumptions, and align outputs with operational reality.

4. Customer enablement

Train teams to understand tooling, read results, and use decisions confidently.
