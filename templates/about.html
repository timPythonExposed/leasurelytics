About Us

We believe planning tools

LeisureLytics builds forecasting, workforce planning, and revenue management solutions for leisure operators modernizing beyond spreadsheets and legacy tools.

Our Mission

Software that plans your operations — not software you have to operate.

Many mid-size leisure companies still rely on Excel, legacy software, or expensive systems that are difficult to adapt. We exist to make that transition realistic.

We use a small, strong team of agentic engineers to accelerate delivery, with humans very much in the loop for design choices, quality control, and customer collaboration. We also prioritize making sure every customer understands the solution they buy.

Our Values

How We Work

Business-first delivery, then technical depth.

1. Understand the case

We start from the business challenge, current process, and practical constraints before writing major code.

2. Rapid PoC with UI

In a short PoC cycle, we deliver a usable interface that shows your data, planning logic, and solution direction early.

3. Production rollout

We convert the PoC into a production-ready planning product, then support adoption so teams can confidently use it day to day.
